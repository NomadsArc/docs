title: "Set Up BOGO Buy X Get Y Discounts in Shopify"
sidebarTitle: "BOGO"
description: "Create Buy X Get Y promotions using Slice 'n Save. Specify which products customers must buy and which they receive discounted, with flexible conditions."
---

The **BOGO (Buy X Get Y)** discount type in Slice 'n Save lets you create classic buy-one-get-one-style promotions — or any variation where buying certain products unlocks a discount on other products. You define the required "buy" products and quantities, the "get" products and their discount, and optionally layer in conditions for cart or customer eligibility.

<Warning>
  BOGO discounts require the **Plus** plan. Upgrade your plan from the Slice 'n Save app settings before setting up this discount type.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new discount">
    Open the [Discounts](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/slice-n-save/discounts) section in the Slice 'n Save app and click **Create discount**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the discount type">
    Choose **Buy X get Y** from the list of discount types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the method and title">
    Select the discount method and enter a title for the discount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add conditions">
    Add one or more conditions to control when the BOGO discount is valid. Conditions can target the cart, line items, or the customer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add criteria">
    Add product-level criteria to filter which products qualify as the "buy" products.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the &quot;Buy&quot; products and quantity">
    Select the products and quantities the customer must add to the cart to trigger the discount. Make sure all required products will be in the cart.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the discount type and value for &quot;Get&quot; products">
    Choose the discount type (percentage or fixed amount) and value that applies to the "get" products.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set discount combinations">
    Choose whether this discount can combine with other Slice 'n Save discounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the discount">
    To activate at a future date or time, update the active dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage markets">
    Select which markets are eligible for this discount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate your discount.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  All "Buy" products must be present in the cart for the BOGO discount to apply. If any required product is missing from the cart, the discount will not activate.
</Note>

<Tip>
  To apply 50% off all red T-shirts when customers buy both blue and red T-shirts: add both T-shirt variants to the "Buy" products list, set the "Get" product to the red T-shirt, and set the discount value to 50%.
</Tip>
