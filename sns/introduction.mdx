Slice 'n Save is a Shopify app that gives you full control over how discounts are applied in your store. Whether you want to offer a percentage off specific products, free shipping when customers spend a certain amount, or complex buy-one-get-one promotions, Slice 'n Save handles it all — without writing a single line of code.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Add Slice 'n Save to your Shopify store from the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/slice-n-save).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a discount">
    Open the Discounts section in the app and choose a discount type that fits your promotion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set conditions">
    Add conditions to control exactly when the discount applies — by cart total, customer tags, product attributes, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and go live">
    Save your discount. It activates automatically at checkout for all qualifying carts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Slice 'n Save discounts are **automatic** — customers never need to enter a discount code. The right discount is applied at checkout based on the conditions you define.
</Note>

## More ways to control your discounts

Beyond discount types and conditions, Slice 'n Save gives you additional tools to run precise, targeted promotions.

- **Scheduling** — Set an active date and time range for any discount so it turns on and off automatically. This is ideal for flash sales, seasonal campaigns, and time-limited offers.
- **Market management** — Choose which of your Shopify Markets a discount applies to, so you can run region-specific promotions without creating separate discount rules for every market.
- **Discount stacking** — Configure whether a Slice 'n Save discount can combine with other Slice 'n Save discounts on the same cart, giving you full control over how promotions interact.
