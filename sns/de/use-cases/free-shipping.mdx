---
title: "Offer Automatic Free Shipping Discounts in Shopify"
sidebarTitle: "Free Shipping"
description: "Set up automatic free shipping on your Shopify store using Slice 'n Save. Apply it to specific shipping rates and control eligibility with conditions."
---

Slice 'n Save lets you offer free shipping automatically at checkout without requiring customers to enter a code. You create a shipping discount, set the value to 100%, and apply conditions — such as a minimum cart total — to control when the free shipping kicks in.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new discount">
    Open the [Discounts](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/slice-n-save/discounts) section in the Slice 'n Save app and click **Create discount**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the discount type">
    Choose **Amount off shipping** from the list of discount types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the method, title, and value to 100%">
    Select your preferred method, enter a title for the discount, and set the discount value to **100%** to make shipping completely free.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add conditions">
    Add one or more conditions to control when free shipping applies. Common conditions include cart total (e.g., cart total ≥ $50), cart country, or customer tags.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select shipping methods">
    Choose the specific shipping rates or methods that the free shipping discount applies to (e.g., Economy, Standard).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set discount combinations">
    Choose whether this discount can combine with other Slice 'n Save discounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the discount">
    To activate at a future date or time, update the active dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage markets">
    Select which markets are eligible for this discount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate your discount.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The most common setup is free shipping for orders over a minimum threshold. Add a single condition — Cart total ≥ your threshold — and select the shipping rates to apply it to.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Free shipping applies automatically at checkout for eligible carts. Customers do not need to enter a discount code.
</Note>
