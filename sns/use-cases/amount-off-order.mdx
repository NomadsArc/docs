The **Amount off order** discount type applies a fixed amount or percentage discount to the entire order total. Unlike product-level discounts, this discount type does not require product criteria — instead, you control eligibility through cart, customer, and line-item conditions.

## Steps Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new discount">
    Open the [Discounts](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/slice-n-save/discounts) section in the Slice 'n Save app and click **Create discount**.

    <Frame>
      ![101c48b05e 82d9c0cf516f573a6bc2bbdb771d77d0](/images/101c48b05e-82d9c0cf516f573a6bc2bbdb771d77d0.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the discount type">
    Choose **Amount off order** from the list of discount types.

    <Frame>
      ![C19361ab54 A992346ec7aea7e855a3cd32ffdab3b5](/images/c19361ab54-a992346ec7aea7e855a3cd32ffdab3b5.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the method, title, and value">
    Select the discount method (percentage or fixed amount), enter a title for the discount, and set the discount value.

    <Frame>
      ![E4adb92d1e E96985d302691ce89a35809c1ed162c0](/images/e4adb92d1e-e96985d302691ce89a35809c1ed162c0.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add conditions">
    Add one or more conditions to control when this discount is valid. You can use cart conditions ( total, subtotal, country, language, attributes ), line-item conditions ( product title, vendor, tags ), or customer type ( lifetime spending, tags, B2B/B2C type ).

    <Frame>
      ![87c31a0e24 68d4b2a96153bfecf8980240873a671b](/images/87c31a0e24-68d4b2a96153bfecf8980240873a671b.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set discount combinations">
    Discounts can be applied in **combination** with other discounts which are created using Slice 'n Save app.

    <Frame>
      ![49d376e4a1 E28a243a85fbc099d0f8f6303fcb3e6b](/images/49d376e4a1-e28a243a85fbc099d0f8f6303fcb3e6b.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the discount">
    To activate the discount at a future date or time, update the active dates in the **Active dates** section.

    <Frame>
      ![49d376e4a1 E28a243a85fbc099d0f8f6303fcb3e6b 1](/images/49d376e4a1-e28a243a85fbc099d0f8f6303fcb3e6b-1.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage markets">
    Adjust the markets from the **manage markets** section for discount eligibility.

    <Frame>
      ![8db261198f 4cd849f7844d2fe7446916a70a20ad5b](/images/8db261198f-4cd849f7844d2fe7446916a70a20ad5b.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate your discount.

    <Frame>
      ![Db738b6efb A443756df4bdc9044a2bf456a5bb9558](/images/db738b6efb-a443756df4bdc9044a2bf456a5bb9558.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips & Gotchas

<Note>
  Order-level discounts do not use product **criteria** — conditions alone determine whether the discount applies. If you need to restrict the discount to specific products, use an **Amount off products** discount instead.
</Note>

<Tip>
  To give B2B customers a 10% order discount when they spend over $500, add two conditions: Customer type = B2B and Cart total ≥ $500.
</Tip>
