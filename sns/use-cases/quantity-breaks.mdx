---
title: "Set Quantity Break Pricing for Products in Shopify"
sidebarTitle: "Quantity Breaks"
description: "Configure quantity break discounts in Slice 'n Save with min/max breakpoints. Reward customers who buy in bulk with automatic pricing tiers."
---

Quantity breaks let you set different discount levels based on the quantity of a product in the cart, using minimum and maximum quantity breakpoints. Unlike tiered discounts (which apply one tier to the whole order), quantity breaks can apply different discounts to different quantity ranges simultaneously — ideal for wholesale pricing and clearance sales.

<Warning>
  Quantity break discounts require the **Plus** plan. Upgrade your plan from the
  Slice 'n Save app settings before setting up this discount type.
</Warning>

## Steps Guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new discount">
    Open the [Discounts](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/slice-n-save/discounts)
    section in the Slice 'n Save app and click **Create discount**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the discount type">
    Choose **Quantity breaks** from the list of discount types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the method and title">
    Select the discount method and enter a title for the discount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add breakpoints">
    Add multiple breakpoints. For each breakpoint, set the minimum quantity,
    maximum quantity, and the discount value. For example: 1–3 items = 10% off,
    4–10 items = 15% off, 11–20 items = 25% off.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add conditions">
    Add conditions to control when the discount is valid — for example, restrict
    it to products tagged "clearance sale".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add criteria">
    Add product-level criteria to filter which products the breakpoints apply to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set discount combinations">
    Choose whether this discount can combine with other Slice 'n Save discounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the discount">
    To activate at a future date or time, update the active dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage markets">
    Select which markets are eligible for this discount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate your discount.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips & Gotchas
<Tip>
  **Clearance sale example:** For products tagged "clearance sale", set three
  breakpoints:

  - 1–3 units → 10% off
  - 4–10 units → 15% off
  - 11–20 units → 25% off
</Tip>

<Info>
  **Quantity breaks** and **tiered discounts** work differently:

  - **Quantity breaks**: Each breakpoint has both a minimum AND maximum quantity
    range. The discount for that range applies to the matching quantity in the
    cart.
  - **Tiered discounts**: Each tier has only a minimum threshold. The highest
    matching tier applies to the whole order.
</Info>
