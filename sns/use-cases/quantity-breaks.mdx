Quantity breaks let you set different discount levels based on the quantity of a product in the cart, using minimum and maximum quantity breakpoints. Unlike tiered discounts (which apply one tier to the whole order), quantity breaks can apply different discounts to different quantity ranges simultaneously — ideal for clearance sales.

<Warning>
  Quantity break discounts require the **Plus** plan. Upgrade your plan from the Slice 'n Save app settings before setting up this discount type.
</Warning>

## Steps Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new discount">
    Open the [Discounts](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/slice-n-save/discounts) section in the Slice 'n Save app and click **Create discount**.

    <Frame>
      ![101c48b05e 82d9c0cf516f573a6bc2bbdb771d77d0](/images/101c48b05e-82d9c0cf516f573a6bc2bbdb771d77d0.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the discount type">
    Choose **Quantity breaks** from the list of discount types.

    <Frame>
      ![147b61a6c9 4ebf43f0877d281b7a4b0f342419459f](/images/147b61a6c9-4ebf43f0877d281b7a4b0f342419459f.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the method and title">
    Select the discount method and enter a title for the discount.

    <Frame>
      ![6193fa5bb8 Bb54cc0224047b25452f68332c1673e6](/images/6193fa5bb8-bb54cc0224047b25452f68332c1673e6.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add breakpoints">
    Add multiple breakpoints. For each breakpoint, set the minimum quantity, maximum quantity, and the discount value. For example: 1–3 items = 10% off, 4–10 items = 15% off, 11–20 items = 25% off.

    <Frame>
      ![5db4650178 Ac755ca29114e34dc1340aa01d82613b](/images/5db4650178-ac755ca29114e34dc1340aa01d82613b.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add conditions">
    Add conditions to control when the discount is valid — for example, restrict it to products tagged "clearance sale".

    <Frame>
      ![B3f5133ba9 788200235807a90a9fab1b5ce9b56e8f](/images/b3f5133ba9-788200235807a90a9fab1b5ce9b56e8f.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add criteria">
    Add product-level criteria to filter which products the breakpoints apply to.

    <Frame>
      ![80c56cd296 Fcdbd3a074c11f052589fa912f59c134](/images/80c56cd296-fcdbd3a074c11f052589fa912f59c134.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set discount combinations">
    Choose whether this discount can combine with other Slice 'n Save discounts.

    <Frame>
      ![55a10a53ae 582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a 8](/images/55a10a53ae-582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a-8.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the discount">
    To activate at a future date or time, update the active dates.

    <Frame>
      ![55a10a53ae 582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a 9](/images/55a10a53ae-582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a-9.png)
    </Frame>

  <Step title="Manage markets">
    Adjust the markets from the **manage markets** section for discount eligibility.

    <Frame>
      ![A2d0ee2612 Dc200f9d3e9a662849c7b1454ef693fe](/images/a2d0ee2612-dc200f9d3e9a662849c7b1454ef693fe.png)

## Tips & Gotchas

<Tip>
  **Clearance sale example:** For products tagged "clearance sale", set three breakpoints:

  - 1–3 units → 10% off
  - 4–10 units → 15% off
  - 11–20 units → 25% off
</Tip>

<Info>
  **Quantity breaks** and **tiered discounts** work differently:

  - **Quantity breaks**: Each breakpoint has both a minimum AND maximum quantity range. The discount for that range applies to the matching quantity in the cart.
  - **Tiered discounts**: Each tier has only a minimum threshold. The highest matching tier applies to the whole order.
</Info>
