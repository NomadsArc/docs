The **Amount off products** discount type in Slice 'n Save lets you apply a fixed amount or percentage discount to specific products in a customer's cart. You can target products precisely using criteria like tags, vendor, or SKU, and control eligibility with conditions on the cart, customer, or line items.

## Steps Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new discount">
    Open the [Discounts](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/slice-n-save/discounts) section in the Slice 'n Save app and click **Create discount**.<br />

    <Frame>
      ![101c48b05e 82d9c0cf516f573a6bc2bbdb771d77d0](/images/101c48b05e-82d9c0cf516f573a6bc2bbdb771d77d0.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the discount type">
    Choose **Amount off products** from the list of discount types.

    <Frame>
      ![Ef7acb36ad 2ee6c193d2dfccd15bbd54361e09a208](/images/ef7acb36ad-2ee6c193d2dfccd15bbd54361e09a208.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the method, title, and value">
    Select the discount method (percentage or fixed amount), enter a title for the discount, and set the discount value.

    <Frame>
      ![E03ceecd94 B5a19f66501ccd9334128aecbc77a06b](/images/e03ceecd94-b5a19f66501ccd9334128aecbc77a06b.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add conditions">
    Add multiple **conditions** to handle the validation of the product discount on the cart and checkout page.

    <Frame>
      ![947e0d8024 Eb7810ef16892d133a8764b2f8de254e](/images/947e0d8024-eb7810ef16892d133a8764b2f8de254e.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add criteria">
    Add product-level criteria to filter which products the discount applies to. For example, apply the discount to products with a specific tag or product vendor.

    <Frame>
      ![4ae40fb4af C5afd0136e4ce1993bcac48508d8138d](/images/4ae40fb4af-c5afd0136e4ce1993bcac48508d8138d.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set discount combinations">
    Discounts can be applied in **combination** with other discounts which are created using Slice 'n Save app.

    <Frame>
      ![F8d3722215 3236dc9bf1d525bac4211498d280889d](/images/f8d3722215-3236dc9bf1d525bac4211498d280889d.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the discount">
    To activate the discount at a future date or time, set the active start and end dates in the **Active dates** section.

    <Frame>
      ![225c1f023a 5627c7a7c30fb4098925a92b34f83166](/images/225c1f023a-5627c7a7c30fb4098925a92b34f83166.png)

  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage markets">
    Adjust the markets from the **manage markets** section for discount eligibility.

    <Frame>
      ![B09b416f33 81556fabedb7df8d542e474fcf528e51](/images/b09b416f33-81556fabedb7df8d542e474fcf528e51.png)

  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate your discount.

    <Frame>
      ![Feb7da1f27 Cacd6c3ad998d3d0b134f27fc2f84fba](/images/feb7da1f27-cacd6c3ad998d3d0b134f27fc2f84fba.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips & Gotchas

<Tip>
  Use **product criteria** alongside cart conditions to create highly targeted promotions — for example, offer 20% off all items tagged "summer-sale" only when the cart total exceeds \$75.
</Tip>

<Info>
  **Conditions** and **criteria** serve distinct roles in your discount configuration:

  - **Conditions** validate whether the discount should apply to the cart at all (based on cart totals, customer attributes, etc.).
  - **Criteria** filter which specific products in the cart receive the discount.
</Info>
