---

Tiered discounts in Slice 'n Save let you reward customers with progressively larger discounts as their cart quantity or subtotal increases. Each tier defines a minimum quantity or subtotal threshold and the discount that applies when that threshold is met — perfect for bulk-buy incentives and seasonal sales.

<Warning>
  Tiered discounts require the **Plus** plan. Upgrade your plan from the Slice
  'n Save app settings before setting up this discount type.
</Warning>

## Steps Guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new discount">
    Open the [Discounts](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/slice-n-save/discounts)
    section in the Slice 'n Save app and click **Create discount**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the discount type">
    Choose **Tiered discount** from the list of discount types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the method and title">
    Select the discount method and enter a title for the discount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add tiers">
    Add multiple tiers to define the discount structure. For each tier, set the
    minimum quantity or subtotal threshold and the corresponding discount value.
    The highest tier whose threshold the cart meets will be applied.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add conditions">
    Add one or more conditions to control when the tiered discount is valid.
    Conditions can target cart totals, product attributes, or customer data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add criteria">
    Add product-level criteria to filter which products in the cart count toward
    the tier thresholds. This lets you scope the tier to specific product tags,
    vendors, or other attributes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set discount combinations">
    Choose whether this discount can combine with other Slice 'n Save discounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the discount">
    To activate at a future date or time, update the active dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage markets">
    Select which markets are eligible for this discount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate your discount.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips & Gotchas
<Tip>
  **BFCM sale example:** Apply 10% off when the minimum subtotal of items tagged
  "bfcm sale" is $100. Apply 20% off when the minimum quantity of those items is
  10. Add a third tier at $200 subtotal to unlock 30% off for your top spenders.
</Tip>

<Info>
  When a cart matches multiple tier thresholds, the **highest matching tier** is
  applied — not all tiers simultaneously.
</Info>
