Tiered discounts in Slice 'n Save let you reward customers with progressively larger discounts as their cart quantity or subtotal increases. Each tier defines a minimum quantity or subtotal threshold and the discount that applies when that threshold is met — perfect for bulk-buy incentives and seasonal sales.

<Warning>
  Tiered discounts require the **Plus** plan. Upgrade your plan from the Slice 'n Save app settings before setting up this discount type.
</Warning>

## Steps Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new discount">
    Open the [Discounts](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/slice-n-save/discounts) section in the Slice 'n Save app and click **Create discount**.

    <Frame>
      ![101c48b05e 82d9c0cf516f573a6bc2bbdb771d77d0](/images/101c48b05e-82d9c0cf516f573a6bc2bbdb771d77d0.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the discount type">
    Choose **Tiered discount** from the list of discount types.

    <Frame>
      ![741a04836a 4f7ca22d743f66d3b1b9142aac180c0f](/images/741a04836a-4f7ca22d743f66d3b1b9142aac180c0f.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the method and title">
    Select the discount method and enter a title for the discount.

    <Frame>
      ![B7d675d46c B5140e2d309e37fca90d66b083d45389](/images/b7d675d46c-b5140e2d309e37fca90d66b083d45389.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add tiers">
    Add multiple tiers to define the discount structure. For each tier, set the minimum quantity or subtotal threshold and the corresponding discount value. The highest tier whose threshold the cart meets will be applied.

    <Frame>
      ![C8d01ad376 7079f59a1500d2d8fa59aca812cd99ff](/images/c8d01ad376-7079f59a1500d2d8fa59aca812cd99ff.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add conditions">
    Add one or more conditions to control when the tiered discount is valid. Conditions can target cart totals, product attributes, or customer data.

    <Frame>
      ![813593b3f6 Fa0ed2cd7b1b938063df6b25d8d01cad](/images/813593b3f6-fa0ed2cd7b1b938063df6b25d8d01cad.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add criteria">
    Add product-level criteria to filter which products in the cart count toward the tier thresholds. This lets you scope the tier to specific product tags, vendors, or other attributes.

    <Frame>
      ![9a9e0373f9 66b31cd142cf919b317c346adf6988a3](/images/9a9e0373f9-66b31cd142cf919b317c346adf6988a3.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set discount combinations">
    Choose whether this discount can combine with other Slice 'n Save discounts.

    <Frame>
      ![55a10a53ae 582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a 6](/images/55a10a53ae-582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a-6.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the discount">
    To activate at a future date or time, update the active dates.

    <Frame>
      ![55a10a53ae 582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a 7](/images/55a10a53ae-582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a-7.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage markets">
    Select which markets are eligible for this discount.

    <Frame>
      ![74304b5bd0 5ec0891b564ac498628ffdc04f49ec1e](/images/74304b5bd0-5ec0891b564ac498628ffdc04f49ec1e.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate your discount.

    <Frame>
      ![1246886c68 A48d16b8f19a8918983e3daeea5b1c3e](/images/1246886c68-a48d16b8f19a8918983e3daeea5b1c3e.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips & Gotchas

<Tip>
  **BFCM sale example:** Apply 10% off when the minimum subtotal of items tagged "bfcm sale" is $100. Apply 20% off when the minimum quantity of those items is
  10. Add a third tier at $200 subtotal to unlock 30% off for your top spenders.
</Tip>

<Info>
  When a cart matches multiple tier thresholds, the **highest matching tier** is applied — not all tiers simultaneously.
</Info>
