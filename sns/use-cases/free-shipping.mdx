Slice 'n Save lets you offer free shipping at checkout without requiring customers to enter a code. You create a shipping discount, set the value  and apply conditions — such as a minimum cart total — to control when the free shipping kicks in.

## Steps Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new discount">
    Open the [Discounts](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/slice-n-save/discounts) section in the Slice 'n Save app and click **Create discount**.

    <Frame>
      ![101c48b05e 82d9c0cf516f573a6bc2bbdb771d77d0](/images/101c48b05e-82d9c0cf516f573a6bc2bbdb771d77d0.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the discount type">
    Choose **Amount off shipping** from the list of discount types.

    <Frame>
      ![6241ab601b 7097eb88b0cf87c988cf4a8fc40cac39](/images/6241ab601b-7097eb88b0cf87c988cf4a8fc40cac39.png)
    </Frame>

    Set the method, title, and value to 100%
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select method, title and value as 100% for the free shipping discount.">
    Select your preferred method, enter a title for the discount, and set the discount value to **100%** to make shipping completely free.

    <Frame>
      ![A3e8fd0e47 1fbfa18f60d178cf04f0e650f2cf15b9](/images/a3e8fd0e47-1fbfa18f60d178cf04f0e650f2cf15b9.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add conditions">
    Add one or more conditions to control when free shipping applies. Common conditions include cart total (e.g., cart total ≥ \$50), cart country, or customer lifetime spending.

    <Frame>
      ![B860377efb Eacd03348437144c8e01bbef4f6ddb34](/images/b860377efb-eacd03348437144c8e01bbef4f6ddb34.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select shipping methods">
    Select the **shipping methods** or rates on which the shipping discount will be applied.

    <Frame>
      ![587629bd23 E8bafd1082f0c3dcdd80aac877a03aa9](/images/587629bd23-e8bafd1082f0c3dcdd80aac877a03aa9.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set discount combinations">
    Choose whether this discount can combine with other Slice 'n Save discounts.

    <Frame>
      ![55a10a53ae 582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a](/images/55a10a53ae-582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the discount">
    To activate at a future date or time, update the active dates.

    <Frame>
      ![55a10a53ae 582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a 1](/images/55a10a53ae-582efb7f9817f1a0eb3356975faad75a-1.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage markets">
    Adjust the markets from the **manage markets** section for discount eligibility.

    <Frame>
      ![E9ddf50d40 1fd864c6a2fb384724d5286bd7e81a4a](/images/e9ddf50d40-1fd864c6a2fb384724d5286bd7e81a4a.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate your discount.

    <Frame>
      ![Af42a59d5a Db0010dfab865b037f6e331a6ad432fd](/images/af42a59d5a-db0010dfab865b037f6e331a6ad432fd.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips & Gotchas

<Tip>
  The most common setup is free shipping for orders over a minimum threshold. Add a single condition — Cart total ≥ your threshold — and select the shipping rates to apply it to.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Free shipping applies automatically at checkout for eligible carts. Customers do not need to enter a discount code.
</Note>
