title: "Discount Specific Shipping Rates with Slice 'n Save"
sidebarTitle: "Amount Off Shipping"
description: "Create a partial shipping discount using Slice 'n Save. Apply a fixed or percentage reduction to specific shipping rates with flexible conditions."
---

The **Amount off shipping** discount type lets you reduce the shipping cost for specific rates by a fixed amount or percentage. Unlike the free shipping setup, you control exactly how much of the shipping cost is discounted — useful for promotions like "50% off Standard shipping for B2B customers".

## Steps Guide
<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new discount">
    Open the [Discounts](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/slice-n-save/discounts) section in the Slice 'n Save app and click **Create discount**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the discount type">
    Choose **Amount off shipping** from the list of discount types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the method, title, and value">
    Select the discount method (percentage or fixed amount), enter a title, and set the discount value. For example, enter 50 for a 50% discount on the shipping rate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add conditions">
    Add conditions to target eligibility. Common options include cart country, customer type (B2B/B2C), customer tags, or cart total.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select shipping methods">
    Choose the specific shipping rates or methods that the discount applies to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set discount combinations">
    Choose whether this discount can combine with other Slice 'n Save discounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule the discount">
    To activate at a future date or time, update the active dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage markets">
    Select which markets are eligible for this discount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to activate your discount.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips & Gotchas
<Tip>
  To offer 50% off Standard shipping for B2B customers: set the discount value to 50%, add a condition Customer type = B2B, and select the Standard shipping method.
</Tip>

<Info>
  - **Free shipping**: Set the discount value to **100%** to fully waive the shipping cost.
  - **Amount off shipping rate**: Set any value between 1–99% (or a fixed amount) for a partial shipping discount.
</Info>
