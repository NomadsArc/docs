## Product Discounts

Create product-level discounts with multiple conditions. You can target promotions using a wide range of **cart conditions**, **customer conditions**, and **line item conditions** — including:

- Cart total
- Product title, vendor, and tags
- Customer lifetime spending and customer tags
- Line item attributes, SKU, weight, and more

This flexibility lets you apply the right discount to exactly the right products for exactly the right customers.

---

## Order Discounts

Create order-level discounts with multiple conditions. Order discounts support the same full set of condition types as product discounts:

- **Cart conditions** — such as cart total and cart subtotal
- **Customer conditions** — such as customer tags and customer type
- **Line item conditions** — such as product vendor and product tags

Use order discounts when you want a single promotional rule to reduce the cost of an entire order rather than targeting individual products.

---

## Shipping Discounts

Create shipping-rate discounts with multiple conditions. You can target specific shipping rates — like Economy or Standard — and apply either free shipping or a percentage discount based on qualifying conditions.

**Examples:**

- Apply a Free shipping discount for the **Economy** rate if the cart total is greater than **$50**.
- Apply a **50% shipping discount** for the **Standard** rate if the customer is **B2B**.

---

## BOGO Discounts

Create BOGO (Buy One, Get One) discounts at the product level with multiple conditions. The items you designate as the "get" products must be present in the cart alongside the qualifying "buy" products for the discount to apply.

**Example:**

- Apply a **50% discount** on all red T-shirts if the customer buys both blue T-shirts **and** red T-shirts.

---

## Tiered Discounts

Create tiers based on **quantity** or **subtotal** for order-level discounts with multiple conditions. As a customer's cart grows, they automatically unlock deeper discounts — no coupon codes required.

**Examples:**

- Apply a **10% discount** if the minimum subtotal of products in the cart is **$100** and those products have the `bfcm sale` product tag.
- Apply a **20% discount** if the minimum quantity of products in the cart is **10** and those products have the `bfcm sale` product tag.

---

## Quantity Break Discounts

Create breaks based on **quantity** for order-level discounts with multiple conditions. Each breakpoint defines a minimum and maximum quantity range, so customers receive the appropriate discount tier automatically.

**Examples:**

- Apply a **10% discount** if the quantity of a product is between **1 and 3** and the product has the `clearance sale` tag.
- Apply a **15% discount** if the quantity of a product is between **4 and 10** and the product has the `clearance sale` tag.
- Apply a **25% discount** if the quantity of a product is between **11 and 20** and the product has the `clearance sale` tag.

---

## Discount Scheduling

Every discount type supports an active date and time range. You set a start date/time and an optional end date/time, and Slice 'n Save activates or deactivates the discount automatically — no manual intervention needed. Use scheduling for flash sales, holiday promotions, or any campaign with a defined window.

---

## Market Management

You can scope any discount to one or more of your Shopify Markets. This means you can run a promotion only in specific regions — for example, a 20% order discount only for customers in France — without duplicating your discount rules or writing custom logic.

---

## Discount Stacking

All discount types support discount **combinations** — you can configure a Slice 'n Save discount to stack with other Slice 'n Save discounts on the same cart. Use this to layer a product discount on top of a free-shipping discount, or any other combination that fits your promotional strategy.

<Note>
  Discount stacking controls how Slice 'n Save discounts combine with each other. You configure combining behavior per discount when you create or edit it.
</Note>
