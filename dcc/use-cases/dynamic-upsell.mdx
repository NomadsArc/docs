## Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new customization">
    Create a new [customization](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/dynamic-checkout-customizer/customizations).

    ![](/images/28fda48321.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the Upsell category">
    Choose **Upsell** as the customization category.

    ![](/images/c6e18f14d6.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the Dynamic Upsell type">
    Select **Dynamic** to recommend products automatically using Shopify product metafields.

    ![](/images/937f26f665.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a placement">
    Select **Dynamic Render** from the available placement options.

    ![](/images/fca81e05df.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure display conditions">
    Add one or more **conditions** to control when the upsell is displayed during checkout.

    ![](/images/826203fc96.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the dynamic source">
    Enter the **Product Metafield** that contains your recommended products. Then configure:

    - **Max Products**
    - **Is Source**
    - **Dynamic Conditions**

    ![](/images/d4636245d2.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize the display">
    Configure the **Display Settings** to control how the upsell products appear during checkout.

    ![](/images/231b000c9a.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate the customization">
    Change the **Status** to **Active** to enable the customization.

    ![](/images/3a06dfb2cf.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the priority">
    Configure the **Priority** (0–100) to determine the execution order when multiple customizations are eligible.

    ![](/images/c921b7cf61.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy the Placement ID">
    Open the **Placement** tab and copy the generated **Placement ID**. You'll use this in the Shopify Checkout Editor.

    ![](/images/e26a7e96d6.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save the customization">
    Click **Save** to store your configuration.

    ![](/images/49b014e51f.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the Checkout Editor">
    Go to your Shopify **Settings → Checkout**, select your checkout profile, and click **Customize**.

    ![](/images/ebe8a89b62.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the app block">
    In the Checkout Editor, click **Add app block**.

    ![](/images/e989097815.png)

    <Note>
      Make sure the app block is placed in the same checkout placement you selected in Dynamic Checkout Customizer.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure the app block">
    Select the **Checkout – Upsell Products** app block, drag it to the desired location, and paste the copied **Placement ID**.

    ![](/images/df2974d6cc.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish your changes">
    Click **Save** in the Checkout Editor to publish the customization.

    ![](/images/efb135da69.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Why does it matter?**

  [FameOnCentral](https://fameoncentral.com/) uses the **Dynamic Upsell** customization to recommend relevant products during checkout, helping increase average order value (AOV) by presenting personalized cross-sell opportunities at the right moment.
</Note>
