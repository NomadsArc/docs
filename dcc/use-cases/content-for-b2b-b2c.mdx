title: "📖 Content for B2B / B2C"
---

How to display different image content for **B2B** and **B2C** customers on the Checkout page using the **Notifications -\> Content** option.

<iframe src="https://www.loom.com/embed/edf3eb7c056343e4a51099ce3d6bd45d" frameborder="0" webkitallowfullscreen mozallowfullscreen allowfullscreen width="100%" height="480" />

## Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new customization">
    Create a new [customization](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/dynamic-checkout-customizer/customizations).

    ![](/images/28fda48321.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Notifications category">
    Choose **Notifications** as the customization category.

    ![](/images/86aed9c810.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the Content type">
    Select **Content** from the available notification types to display custom images or text during checkout.

    ![](/images/328784f3c3.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a placement">
    Choose **Dynamic Render** from the available placement options.

    ![](/images/fca81e05df.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure display conditions">
    Add one or more **conditions** to control when the content appears during checkout.

    ![](/images/ce9058af5a.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Target B2B customers">
    To display the content only for B2B customers, add a **Customer Type** condition and select **B2B** as the value.

    ![](/images/8e88290188.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the content">
    Configure the **Content Settings**, including the **Image Source URL** and **Redirect URL** for the content you want to display.

    ![](/images/3ca98b8bc6.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate the customization">
    Change the **Status** to **Active** to enable the customization.

    ![](/images/dfdf8a69ed.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the priority">
    Configure the **Priority** (0–100) to control the execution order when multiple customizations are eligible.

    ![](/images/065de50336.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the Placement ID">
    Open the **Placement** tab and copy the generated **Placement ID**. You'll use this in the Shopify Checkout Editor.

    ![](/images/a76aa11836.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the customization">
    Click **Save** to store your configuration.

    ![](/images/868a548598.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Checkout Editor">
    Go to your Shopify **[Settings](https://admin.shopify.com/settings) → Checkout**, select your checkout profile, and click **Customize**.

    ![](/images/ebe8a89b62.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the app block">
    In the [Checkout Editor](https://admin.shopify.com/settings/checkout/editor), click **Add app block**.

    ![](/images/e989097815.png)

    <Note>
      Make sure the app block is placed in the same checkout placement you selected in Dynamic Checkout Customizer.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the app block">
    Select the **Checkout – Image Content** app block, drag it to the desired location, and paste the copied **Placement ID**.

    ![](/images/6f53fdc188.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish your changes">
    Click **Save** in the Checkout Editor to publish the customization.

    ![](/images/70e849ab70.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Why does it matter?**

  <a href="https://gimaguas.com/">Gimaguas</a> is using uses the **Notifications → Content** customization to display images during checkout, helping strengthen its content marketing strategy while creating a more engaging customer experience.
</Note>
