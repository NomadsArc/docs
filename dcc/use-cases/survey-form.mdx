How to display **Survey Form** for the customers on the Checkout using the **Notifications -\> Form** option.

<iframe src="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1qjim-C2V6yShMHh57Hf9pkQ5qo1Vyf2n/preview" width="640" height="480" allow="autoplay" />

## Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new customization">
    Create a new [customization](https://admin.shopify.com/apps/dynamic-checkout-customizer/customizations).

    ![](/images/28fda48321.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Notifications category">
    Choose **Notifications** as the customization category.

    ![](/images/86aed9c810.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the Form type">
    Select **Form** from the available notification types to display custom form elements during checkout.

    ![](/images/b390a3a55b.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a placement">
    Select **Dynamic Render** from the available placement options.

    ![](/images/fca81e05df.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure display conditions">
    Add one or more **conditions** to control when the form is displayed during checkout.

    ![](/images/c601b5f168.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the survey form">
    Configure the **Form Settings**, including:

    - **Element Type:** Option List
    - **Label** shown to customers
    - **Key** used to save the selected value in the Shopify order
    - **Block Checkout** to require customers to make a selection before completing checkout
    - Add one or more **Options** for customers to choose from

    ![](/images/5656401da0.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate the customization">
    Change the **Status** to **Active** to enable the customization.

    ![](/images/81e27e6113.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the priority">
    Configure the **Priority** (0–100) to determine the execution order when multiple customizations are eligible.

    ![](/images/5656401da0.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the Placement ID">
    Open the **Placement** tab and copy the generated **Placement ID**. You'll use this in the Shopify Checkout Editor.

    ![](/images/2e4d6d963f.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the customization">
    Click **Save** to store your configuration.

    ![](/images/ed7c23bdf9.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Checkout Editor">
    Go to your Shopify **[Settings](https://admin.shopify.com/settings) → Checkout**, select your checkout profile, and click **Customize**.

    ![](/images/ebe8a89b62.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the app block">
    In the [Checkout Editor](https://admin.shopify.com/settings/checkout/editor), click **Add app block**.

    ![](/images/e989097815.png)

    <Note>
      Make sure the app block is placed in the same checkout placement you selected in Dynamic Checkout Customizer.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the app block">
    Select the **Checkout – Form Elements** app block, drag it to the desired location, and paste the copied **Placement ID**.

    ![](/images/1e4bd03a7a.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish your changes">
    Click **Save** in the Checkout Editor to publish the customization.

    ![](/images/7435e6999d.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Why does it matter?**

  [Sunco](https://www.sunco.com/) uses the **Notifications → Form** customization to display custom survey forms during checkout, allowing the business to collect valuable customer feedback while improving customer engagement and strengthening brand reputation.
</Note>
