## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Install Dynamic Checkout Customizer on your Shopify Plus store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a customization">
    Select the checkout element you want to add, such as an upsell, banner, trust badge, custom field, or informational content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure display rules">
    Customize the appearance and define when and where your checkout content should appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish your changes">
    Save your configuration and publish it to enhance your checkout experience.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Dynamic Checkout Customizer allows you to personalize your Shopify checkout without editing checkout code, making it easy to launch new promotions, collect customer information, and improve conversions.
</Note>

## Popular use cases

Whether you're looking to increase revenue or improve customer confidence, Dynamic Checkout Customizer supports a wide range of checkout enhancements.

- **Increase AOV** with dynamic and static upsell offers.
- **Promote campaigns** using banners and promotional messages.
- **Build trust** with Trustpilot review images, trust badges, and supported payment icons.
- **Collect customer information** through custom fields, surveys, and order notes.
- **Improve checkout usability** with quantity selectors, address validation, and product tags.
- **Provide clarity** by displaying shipping, customs, and policy information directly in checkout.
- **Personalize content** for different customer groups, including B2B and B2C buyers.
